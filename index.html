Getting Started - Piano

Before beginning to learn to play music on piano, there are some key musical theories that must be known. These same concepts are used by novices and professionals alike. Initially, it may seem like a lot of information to learn just to be able to play but knowing these initial concepts will only enhance the quality and sound of the music you create.
